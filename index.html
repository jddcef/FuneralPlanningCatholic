Understanding the Catholic Funeral

The Vigil / Wake

A prayer service held the evening before the funeral, often at a funeral home or church.

The Funeral Mass

The central liturgical celebration with readings, prayers, and Eucharist.

The Burial / Committal

Final prayers at the gravesite or mausoleum, committing the body to its final resting place.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between a vigil and a funeral mass?

The vigil is a prayer service held the evening before the funeral, while the funeral mass is the main liturgical celebration.

How many readings should be selected?

Typically, you'll need one First Reading, one Psalm, one Second Reading, and one Gospel reading.

Can I customize the funeral program?

Yes, you can download our editable templates and customize them according to your needs.
